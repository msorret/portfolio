UX Research Internship

From September of 2017 to August of 2018, I worked as a UX/UI intern for the Human Factors Engineering Department of the U.S. Department of Veteran Affairs. I worked with the departments UX Designer, primarily on developing a UX guide and conducting usability walkthroughs on mobile products.

The UX guide covered guidelines for other departments to follow, on building personas, user journeys, and principles of UX design.

The major product that I worked on was an internal application for mental health professionals to streamline the process of admitting a patient. For this project, I recorded data during usability walkthroughs of the original design, organized and analyzed the data, and developed slide decks to present findings and suggestions.
